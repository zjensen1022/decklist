Standard fevered-thing

This pauper deck is fairly competitive, but still cheap (even for pauper). The deck is centered around Nantuko husk and bloodthrone vampire. Many of the creaures has an abilty on death for good sacrifice. The deck has high amounts of synergy, and most importantly it's fun to play

Deck

  • 4x Nantuko husk
  • 4x bloodthrone vampire
  • 3x eyeless watcher
  • 2x dutiful attendent
  • 4x elvish visionary
  • 4x alter's reap
  • 4x blisterpod
  • 4x sanitarium skeleton
  • 4x shambling goblin
  • 4x tragic slip
  • 3x hunger of the howlpack

Lands

  • 4x Jungle Hallow
  • 2x golgari guildgate
  • 6x forest
  • 8x swamp

Sideboard

  • 1x hunger of the howlpack
  • 4x festering newt
  • 3x bone splinters
  • 3x festerhide boar
  • 4x natural state

sideboarding

natural state is a nice way to fight affinity, as it can get rid of almost any artifact and interupt the tempo of the deck, and it's only 1 mana. 4 festering newt, and 4 shambling goblin go great against tokens and white weenies. festerhide boar is nice because it's often a 5/5 trample for 4 that can get around small creatures.
